About the Product

  • Incident Intel is a web application that allows users to report incidents that they have witnessed or experienced.
  • Users can report incidents such as accidents, crimes, and other emergencies, and provide details such as the location, time, and description of the incident.
  • The application also allows users to view incidents reported by other users on a map, and filter incidents based on location, time, and type.
  • Incident Intel aims to provide a platform for users to share information about incidents in their community, and help improve public safety and awareness.
