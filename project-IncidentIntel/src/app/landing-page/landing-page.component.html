Incident Intel

About the Agent

Incident Intel is an AI-powered agent designed to streamline issue resolution by providing valuable insights from historical incident data. By analyzing past JIRA tickets, it delivers key information such as the count of similar issues, relevant solutions or resolutions, and associated comments or queries.

The agent also highlights any dependency stories related to the given keywords, ensuring you have all the context you need to address current challenges efficiently. With Incident Intel, you can leverage the power of AI to resolve issues faster, learn from past incidents, and improve overall productivity.
